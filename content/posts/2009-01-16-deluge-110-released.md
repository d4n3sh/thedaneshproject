Deluge 1.1.0 is out.

Some of the major changes in the release;

Labels.  
Proxy support.  
Authentication for the daemon.  
Per-tracker filtering.  
Magnet uri/hash.  
Improved file tabs with file/folder renaming functions.  
QOS support.  
For the full change list go to the [changelog][1].
